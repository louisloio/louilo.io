Sneaker War

War rules

Brief and Benchmark

The gameplay will be based on Candy Crush.
22 different pairs of sneakers will be used in the game.

Target to win

score a maximum of points by aligning 3 or more shoes to make them blow and generate some points.


Basic gameplay

The duration of each game is limited to 3 minutes.
Align 3 or more sneakers to blow them up and score xxx points vertically or horizontally.
When 4, 5 or 6 shoes are blown up, it creates a Magic ball that will randomly jump through the board and destroys sneakers.
4 shoes: it creates a bronze magic ball that will destroy 1/3 of the sneakers on the board
5 shoes: it creates a silver magic ball that will destroy 1/2 of the sneakers on the board
6 shoes: it creates a gold magic ball that will destroy all the sneakers on the board
The basketball could also be created by a square of 4 identical sneakers.

When the architecture and scenario is completed then I start to make a low fidelety prototype for each case of my userjourney
